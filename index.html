About Us

For more than 18 years, Amy Global Engineering has been the contractor to various Factories, commercial building and has been providing our quality services over the past year. We are fully licensed specialist in all fabrication and installation of interior and exterior ornamental metal works projects. Amy Global Engineering is proudly offering solutions for commercial and residential welding, we provide the highest quality welding with the best and good quality metal for metal fabrication works and manufacturing services. Our experienced welders of metal fabrication are professionals and preferred work hand in hand with you in order to ensure that your idea design is executed to perfection.

Be the best and quality contractor and to be the first choice in this field.
